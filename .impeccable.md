## Design Context

### Users
- Primary users are admins and moderators managing community content, shop data, orders, user approvals, payments, and submissions.
- Secondary users are fans and community members browsing updates, sending support, joining the community, and viewing content.
- Tertiary users are merch buyers who need a clear storefront and order flow.
- Admin screens should optimize for repeated daily operational work: quick scanning, filtering, editing, approving, and reviewing records with minimal friction.

### Brand Personality
- Pragmatic, clear, compact.
- The interface should feel like a modern SaaS/admin tool rather than a sentimental fandom microsite.
- Information density is more important than decorative warmth.
- The experience should feel calm, efficient, legible, and reliable for moderators under routine operational load.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Default direction is light and soft, with white or near-white backgrounds and neutral surfaces.
- Accent color use should be restrained, with orange reserved primarily for alerts, warnings, and important attention states.
- The UI reference is the feel of the admin in `mipocket`: table-first, utility-led, persistent sidebar, compact controls, practical toolbars, and polished but understated surfaces.
- This project should not prioritize overt warmth, fandom sentimentality, or strong expressive brand identity in admin views.
- Admin UI decisions should favor scanability, density, and operational clarity over ornamental visuals.

### Design Principles
1. Table-first operations: design admin pages around searchable, paginated, action-oriented data tables.
2. Dense but controlled: keep controls compact and information-rich without feeling cramped or chaotic.
3. Neutral-first hierarchy: use light neutrals as the dominant visual field and reserve accent color for meaningful status or urgency.
4. Utility over decoration: every visual treatment should improve comprehension, prioritization, or speed of use.
5. Standard strong accessibility: preserve keyboard support, visible focus states, solid contrast, and reduced-motion-respectful interactions.
